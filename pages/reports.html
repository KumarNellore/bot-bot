bot-bot by Imaginea
Selenium like automation framework for native android applications.

bot-bot currently generates three types of reports for runner execution.
You can click on the following links to see the sample reports that are generated:

  1. TestNG default under "test-report/TestNG-report"
  2. Junit under "test-report/Junit"
  3. TestNG-xslt under "test-report/TestNG-XSLT-report"
